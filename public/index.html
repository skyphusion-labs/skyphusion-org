Operating principles

How we work

  1. 01
    Own it over rent it. Your GPU, your keys, your data. We build for sovereignty, not convenience fees.
  2. 02
    Make the thing. Ship working software. Don't just monetize the idea of shipping.
  3. 03
    AGPL by default. If you run it, you can read it. If you improve it, the community gets those improvements.
  4. 04
    Edge-native, not edge-washed. Cloudflare Workers aren't a deployment target; they're the architecture.
  5. 05
    Kill the cop in your head. Permission was never required. Fork it. Break it. Make it yours.
